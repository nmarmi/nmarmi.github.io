About Me

Hi, I am Nicolò Marmi, I'm a student at Stony Brook Univieristy pursuing a double major in Honors Computer Science and Applied Mathematics.

Since I was young I've alwasy been fascinated by tech. When I was 9 I saw my dad throwing away our old computer, so I asked him if we could take it apart together (and we did, it was a lot of fun!). When I was 11 I asked for Lego Mindstorms for christmas, and built a robot that could follow the path I laid out with black tape. When I was 13 I built my first game using scratch. Finally, at 17 I started learning python.
